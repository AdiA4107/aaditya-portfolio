Educate India

I launched Educate India as a portal to spread awareness on opportunities to obtain Scholarships and Loans for higher education among Indian students. I authored the Educate India Handbook, conducted seminars through the website, and hosted podcasts to guide students through the application process.

Visit the Educate India website!

Key Features

EMI Calculator

A comprehensive EMI calculator that helps students calculate the cost of education loans for financial planning.

Scholarship Finder

A database of scholarships tailored for different university types and student backgrounds to maximize funding opportunities.

Loan Hub

A comprehensive resource featuring public and private loan options with detailed comparisons of their advantages and disadvantages.
